Privacy Policy

This application connects with Etsy and EverBee APIs to provide seller analytics, SEO optimization, listing research, and AI-powered productivity features.

The app may access store, product, and analytics data authorized by the user. No customer payment information is stored.

Data is used solely for analytics, optimization, and internal productivity functionality. The application does not sell personal information or interact with buyer storefronts.

Users may revoke access at any time by disconnecting the application from their Etsy or EverBee account.
